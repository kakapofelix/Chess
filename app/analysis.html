Why game analyzing is so important?

How to be a better player?

If you want to be a successful player, or a good one at least, always remember three very important words: analysing, analysing and analysing. Oh sorry, that was four words actually.

I bet many chess player do not analyze their games. Well, not a surprise that it is not a good practice. Whether you just won or lost, you want to move on, concentrate and prepare for your next game. Believe me, I want it, too and I know it is hard to sit down and spend time with analyzing your game you just lost, BUT you have to do this! So now the big question is how to analyze your game? I can give you some tips:

#1 write notes immediately after the game. Just few words to help recall the thoughts later.

#2 pay attention to each movement and reconsider the decision. You have to understand your movements.

#3 remember when you were hezitating. Think about that position and look at your options. Why were you hezitating? You have to answer your questions.

#4 memorize and practice certain situations when you are not quite sure about.
